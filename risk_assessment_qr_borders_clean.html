MS70 Low

RW mechanical failure (bearings wear, friction increase, seizure) causing performance degradation (reduced toques, increased jitter)

CategoryMission ProbabilityC Severity3
Mitigation

- Choose a RW with high operational life
- Space qualified components
- High-reliability bearings
- Conservative operational envelope (limit speed/torque)
- Redundancy
- Stressfull testing (development, acceptance and qualification tests)

Note: Probability of occurance is not so low, RWs are known to undergo failures so modern design are getting better to prevent this situation
MS80 Low

RW electronic failure (short circuits in winding, open circuit, power supply failure) causing performance degradation (reduced toques, increased jitter)

CategoryMission ProbabilityC Severity3
Mitigation

- Space qualified components
- Radiation-hardened electronics
- Current and temperature monitoring with auto shutdown
- Redundancy

MS90 Low

RW electronic failure due to overheating causing performance degradation (reduced toques, increased jitter)

CategoryMission ProbabilityB Severity4

MS100 Medium

Multiple RWs failure causing loss of 3-axis control

CategoryMission ProbabilityB Severity5

TC20 Low

RW saturation/inefficiency (momentum dumping)

CategoryTechnical ProbabilityC Severity3
Mitigation

- Integration of a desaturation loop with ADCS thrusters in the actuator plant
- Regular and planned momentum dumping strategy
- Conservative RW operational limits

Note: Even with mitigation, momentum accumulation is expected during long mission operations. However, having actions to activate to desaturate, the severity is not extremely high.

MS110 Low

CategoryMission ProbabilityA Severity5

Risk count 15

EPS

TC60 Low

Hypervelocity impacts (MMOD Strike) shatter individual coverglasses or several vital interconnect wire.

CategoryTechnical ProbabilityC Severity2
Mitigation

Highly parallelized circuit topologies (multiple short strings rather than fewer long strings) so losing a localized area doesn't drop the whole array's voltage.

Note: No dangerous debris environment in moon orbit
MS120 Very low

Single Event Bornout: Heavy ions or cosmic rays trigger a destructive short circuit in power MOSFETs.

CategoryMission ProbabilityA Severity4
Mitigation

Oversizing, using radiation-hardened components, high voltage/current derating (running loads well beload max ratings)

Note: No dangerous radiation exposition in moon orbit
MS130 Low

SADA / SADM Mechanical Jam: The Solar Array Drive Assembly/Mechanism stalls, preventing the panels from sun-tracking.

CategoryMission ProbabilityB Severity3
Mitigation

- Redundant motor windings
- Dual-channel bearing systems
- "Safe mode" attitude profile that aligns the entire spacecraft bus to compensate for a fixed panel

MS140 Medium

Mechanism sticking or dampener failure leaves panels stowed or partially deployed.

CategoryMission ProbabilityB Severity5
Mitigation

- High mechanical margin in spring actuators
- Redundant burn-wire/pin-puller release mechanisms
- Operational maneuvers to shake free

MS150 Low

Battery Thermal Runaway: Internal short circuit or overcharging causes catastrophic heat release.

CategoryMission ProbabilityA Severity5
Mitigation

- Strict charge/discharge monitoring at the Battery Management System (BMS) level
- Independent hardware-level overvoltage cutoff switches

TC70 Low

Battery Capacity Fading: Repeated cycling (especially in low Earth orbit) reduces total Wh capacity.

CategoryTechnical ProbabilityC Severity3
Mitigation

- Restricting the operational Depth of Discharge (DoD) to a conservative range
- Active cell balancing

TC80 Low

Solar Cell Degradation (Atomic Oxygen/Radiation): Space environment degrades power output over time faster than expected.

CategoryTechnical ProbabilityD Severity2
Mitigation

- Over-sizing the solar array at Beginning of Life (BOL) to guarantee required End of Life (EOL) power
- Protective fused silica coverglass

MS160 Medium

Shunt Regulator / PCU Failure: The Power Control Unit fails, causing either an unregulated high-voltage spike or loss of bus regulation.

CategoryMission ProbabilityB Severity5
Mitigation

Redundant topology

TC90 Low

Solar Array Partial Shadowing: Spacecraft structure blocks sunlight, causing localized heating (hot spots) and string power loss.

CategoryTechnical ProbabilityC Severity2
Mitigation

- Bypass diodes across cell strings to allow current to bypass shadowed sections
- Optimized attitude control maneuvers

MS170 Low

Electrolyte Leakage / Cell Venting: Vacuum exposure or internal pressure build-up causes a seal breach, venting toxic or corrosive gas inside the bus.

CategoryMission ProbabilityB Severity4
Mitigation

- Battery pack hermetically sealed
- Internal pressure relief mechanisms that direct vented gas away from sensitive avionics

EN20 Low

Electrolyte Leakage / Cell Venting: Vacuum exposure or internal pressure build-up causes a seal breach, releasing toxic or corrosive gas in the environment.

CategoryEnvironment ProbabilityB Severity4
Mitigation

- Battery pack hermetically sealed

MS180 Low

Structural Interconnect Failure: Launch vibrations and acoustic loads break the wire bonds or busbars connecting the cells together.

CategoryMission ProbabilityA Severity5
Mitigation

- Designed for high random vibration loads
- Potting cell matrix with space-grade silicone/epoxy
- Flexible wire bonding
- Cabling stressfully tested at subsystem and system-level against vibrations

MS190 Low

Heater Circuit Failure (Over-cooling): During eclipse, battery heaters fail to turn on, dropping temperatures below freezing thresholds where chemistry freezes.

CategoryMission ProbabilityB Severity4
Mitigation

- Fully redundant heater strings with independent thermostats
- Multi-layer insulation (MLI) blankets around the battery pack

TC100 Medium

Reverse Biasing "Hot Spot" Destruction: Partial shadowing turns a shaded cell into a consumer of power rather than a producer, leading to localized overheating.

CategoryTechnical ProbabilityC Severity4
Mitigation

Bypass diodes in parallel with small groups of cells to provide an alternate low-resistance path for the string current when shaded
